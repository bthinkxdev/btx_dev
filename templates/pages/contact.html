FAQ

Common Questions

How long does a typical project take?+
It depends on the scope. A basic web application takes 4-8 weeks. A full e-commerce platform or SaaS product typically takes 8-16 weeks. We'll give you a precise timeline after the discovery call.
Do you work with international clients?+
Yes, 100%. We work with clients across Europe, North America, the Middle East, and beyond. All communication is in English, and we're used to async collaboration across time zones.
What's your pricing model?+
We offer fixed-price projects for well-scoped work, and time-and-materials engagements for longer, evolving products. We'll recommend the right model during the scoping call.
Will I own the code and IP?+
Absolutely. Upon full payment, all source code, assets, and intellectual property are fully transferred to you. We include this in every contract, no exceptions.
Do you provide ongoing support after launch?+
Yes. We offer monthly retainer packages for maintenance, feature development, and technical support. We also provide a 30-day bug fix guarantee on all projects after launch.
Can you work with an existing codebase?+
Yes. We frequently take on existing Django, React, or Node.js projects to add features, fix issues, or scale the architecture. We'll start with a code audit to understand what you have.
